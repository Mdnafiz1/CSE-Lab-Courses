Welcome to Chandpur

Chandpur is a beautiful district in Bangladesh, known for its mesmerizing rivers, especially the confluence of the Padma, Meghna, and Dakatia rivers. The city is famous for its rich cultural heritage, bustling markets, and fresh Hilsa (Ilish) fish. Whether you are a history lover or a food enthusiast, Chandpur has something for everyone.

Historical Places in Chandpur

  1. Meghna Bridge
  2. Chandpur Science and Technology University
  3. Kachua Shahi Mosque
  4. Faridganj Zamindar House

Popular Foods in Chandpur

  • Hilsa Fish (Ilish)
  • Pitha (Traditional Rice Cakes)
  • Chandpur Sweets
  • Fresh River Fish Curries
